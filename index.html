ABOUT ME

I'm thrilled to have you on my personal space. As an acclaimed multidisciplinary creative, I specialize in telling stories through writing for stage and screen. I am passionate about showcasing the beauty and diversity of African culture. With each project, I aim to bring the richness of African drama, films, and TV productions to a global audience, bridging cultures and sparking conversations through the power of storytelling.

MY WORKS

THE DEVIL'S SONG

Ashipa is loved and trusted by the king, his bosom friend, who, on becoming king, made him the traditional prime minister. However, Ashipa desires absolute power. He eventually takes advantage of his position to seize power. He becomes a tyrant and misrules the land. He destroys all rivals and compels absolute loyalty. He is unyielding and vows to die in power.

DANCE OF THE SACRED FEET

Adeyemi is the choice of the people as king. However, before he can be crowned king, he dies in very mysterious circumstances. To the chagrin of Araba, Adeyemi's rival to the throne, Adeyemi's 12-year-old son is crowned. Araba and his cohorts vow not to accept Adeyemi's son as king. They ultimately seize power and force the young king and his mother into exile. This causes a prolonged hardship on the people.

MY WORKS

AYINLA

Ayinla is a musical eponymous film based on the life of the late Nigerian music maestro Ayinla Yusuf popularly known as Ayinla Omowura, an Apala musician who was stabbed to death by his manager named Bayewu in a bar fight on May 6, 1980 at Abeokuta.

My Creative Vision

I believe that storytelling has the power to change perceptions, inspire empathy, and break down barriers. With this belief as my guiding light, I pour my heart and soul into crafting narratives that reflect the essence of African life and its multifaceted beauty.

Multidisciplinary Approach

As a multidisciplinary creative, I explore various mediums to express my artistic vision fully. From traditional theatre to cutting-edge cinema and gripping TV productions, I embrace each opportunity to push boundaries and challenge conventional norms.

Collaborations And Impacts

Throughout my career, I have been fortunate to collaborate with a diverse array of talented individuals across the entertainment industry. These collaborations have not only enriched my work but have also allowed me to amplify the voices of fellow artists and create a lasting impact on the communities we touch.
